FAQ

Common questions.

Everything you need to know before downloading.

Is it really free? What's the catch?
No catch. ING Listing Engine is freeware — no subscription, no expiry, no feature gating. It was built to solve a real problem and released free. You do need your own Claude AI API key (Anthropic offers free tier credits) and your own eBay seller account.
What do I need to get started?
Windows 10 or 11, a free Anthropic Claude API key (for AI features), and an eBay seller account. The app walks you through connecting both during first-time setup. No install — just double-click the .exe file.
Does it work for products outside of mining hardware?
Yes. The AI adapts to any product category — electronics, tools, clothing, collectibles, auto parts, sporting goods. The item specifics it fills will match whatever eBay category the product belongs to.
Is my eBay account information secure?
Your credentials are stored locally in a credentials.json file on your own PC — they never leave your machine and are not sent to any third-party server. eBay authentication uses the official OAuth flow.
Can I edit the AI-generated listing before publishing?
Absolutely. Everything the AI fills is fully editable before you publish. The AI gives you a strong starting point — you review, tweak, and approve before anything goes live on eBay.
How does the bulk import work?
Paste a collection or category page URL (e.g., a supplier's product grid page). The app detects all product links on the page and processes each one in parallel. Each product opens as its own draft tab with AI-filled fields, ready to review and publish one by one.
What eBay marketplaces are supported?
The app uses the eBay Sell API and currently targets eBay US (ebay.com). eBay sandbox mode is also supported for testing without creating live listings.
Where can I get help or report bugs?
Open an issue on the GitHub repository at github.com/essquireo0o/ING-eBay-Autolister — or email ING Mining directly. Active development continues and bug reports are taken seriously.
